Study Strategy Notes

This planner is designed as a rapid front-end prototype to support focused learning. It uses simple rules of thumb: short blocks, clear priorities, and a quick visual load indicator.

  • Keep blocks between 25–50 minutes to avoid burnout.
  • Mix one high-priority block with one lighter task.
  • Use the load rating as a reality check: don’t overload the day.
